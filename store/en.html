FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is GridWidget?

GridWidget replaces your Chrome new tab page with a fully customizable personal dashboard. You get 19 widgets — clock, calendar, tasks, weather, stocks, notes, habits, focus timer — arranged exactly how you want them.

Is it really free? No catch?

Yes — all 19 widgets are free forever with no ads, no subscriptions, and no data collection. Pro ($1.99/mo) adds Notion sync and priority support, but you never need it for the core dashboard.

Why does it ask for Google permissions?

Google permissions are only requested if you choose to connect Calendar, Tasks, or Drive. GridWidget uses secure OAuth — we never see your password, never send your data to our servers. Revoke anytime at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Can I customize the layout and look?

Absolutely. Drag widgets anywhere, resize them by dragging corners, lock the layout. Choose from 10+ background presets or use your own image URL. Widget accent colors and sizes are all customizable.

Will settings sync across my devices?

Yes — your layout syncs across Chrome installations via Chrome's built-in sync. You can also Export a full backup JSON and Import it anywhere, or on any browser profile.

Privacy First

Your data is yours

GridWidget never collects, sends, or sells your data. Everything stays in your browser and your own Google account.

Google Calendar
calendar.events

Shows events and lets you add new ones from the dashboard. Never sent to GridWidget servers.

Google Tasks
tasks

Displays task lists and lets you check items off. Data stays in your Google Tasks account only.

Google Drive
drive.file

Only accesses files created by GridWidget. Cannot read your other Drive files.

Profile / Email
userinfo

Used only to display your signed-in account name on screen. Never stored or transmitted.

Full details in our Privacy Policy. Revoke Google access anytime at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
