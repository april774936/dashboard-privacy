Your data is yours

GridWidget does not collect, transmit, or sell your data. Everything stays in your browser and your own Google account.

Google Calendar
calendar.events

Shows upcoming events and lets you add or edit them. Events stay in your Google account only — never sent to any server.

Google Tasks
tasks

Shows your task lists and lets you add and complete tasks. Data stays in Google Tasks only.

Google Drive
drive.file

Accesses only files the notes widget creates. Cannot access any other files in your Drive.

Profile / Email
userinfo

Used only to display which account you are signed in with.

See the Privacy Policy for details. Revoke access anytime from Google Account permissions.
